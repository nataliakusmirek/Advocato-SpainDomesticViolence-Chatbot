OUR STORY

Our work at Advocato is to achieve one purpose: make things that aren't serving communities effectively, better. Initially starting as a project idea for the United Nation's SDG GameChanger Awards in 2024, we began to realize that we couldn't just stop after one project. We could keep making, building, and improving.
And that's what we do.
